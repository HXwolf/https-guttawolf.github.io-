BIO

GUTTAWOLF is the collaborative force of Guttamouf, an NYC emcee known for raw lyricism and punchline-heavy flows, and theBigBudWolf, a Canadian producer crafting dark, hard-hitting boom bap beats. Together, they form a gritty, primal sound that blends street-rooted hip-hop with eerie, cinematic undertones — bridging classic cipher energy with the decentralized future.

Also known as The Scream Team, Raze & Lucien, Tussle Simmons and Tricky Rubin (depending on the moon phase), this duo doesn't just drop tracks — they summon terror and fright.

It's not just a duo — GUTTAWOLF is a sound, a symbol, and a movement: Boom bap from the shadows. Blockchain from the underground. The pack runs deep.
